About Me

Hello! I'm James, a passionate cybersecurity professional with a strong technical background in telecommunications and IT infrastructure. With years of hands-on experience in the industry, I've made it my mission to provide top-notch IT support services to homes and businesses in our local community.

My Journey in Tech

My journey in technology began at BT, where I worked as a Telephone, TV, and Broadband Engineer. This role gave me invaluable experience in:

  • Installing and maintaining complex telecommunications systems
  • Troubleshooting network and connectivity issues
  • Delivering exceptional customer service in high-pressure situations
  • Working with a wide range of networking equipment and technologies

Cybersecurity Expertise

Driven by my passion for cybersecurity, I pursued and earned a degree in the field, equipping me with:

  • In-depth knowledge of network security principles and best practices
  • Expertise in threat detection and prevention
  • Experience with security auditing and compliance
  • Understanding of ethical hacking techniques

Why Choose My Services?

What sets me apart is my unique combination of telecommunications expertise and cybersecurity knowledge. I understand both the physical and digital aspects of IT infrastructure, allowing me to provide comprehensive solutions that are not only effective but also secure by design.

I take pride in offering personalised, reliable IT support with the professionalism you'd expect from a large company, combined with the personal touch of a local service provider. Whether you're a home user needing basic tech support or a business looking to secure your digital assets, I'm here to help.
